About Our Project

What is PAKA? A Parking App

It is a parking app that allows our users to book for parking space of their desired destination online. They will then be given directions to the location they booked through navigation on the provided map of the app. Upon arrival they will confirm their arrival through any IOT (Internet of Things) technology provided by the parking adminstration. The information system has an adminstration website which will be used by our clients, the website will handle the adminstration of parking. Therefore on the website our clients will be able to register a parkings, update the parkinsg and add the technologies used to identify cars in their parking facilities. They will be able to see their analytics, daily business profits and projections as well
