    About Our Project

    We as Genesis have built a system that allows car owners to reserve parking spaces. We created an application that allows our users to select an appropriate parking space of their desired parking destination by booking it. They will then be given directions to the location they booked through navigation on the provided map of the app. Upon arrival they will confirm their arrival with QR Code scanning to confirm that they have arrived. Their timer will then start after parking their vehicle and then they will be notified when their time is close to ending and they will be given an extension option should they choose to do so and if not they can pay throught the app and recieve their invoice after exiting the premises.

    With Our App

    You have control of your own parking spot in the palms of your fingers

    The Problem

    Drivers spend a considerable amount of time looking for a parking space, and in today's hectic work world, while non of them has time to waste looking for parking space. The standard way of locating parking by observation or relying on parking attendants has a host of drawbacks.

    *

    The Solution

    The Solution is Paka, An App We developed. It is the vehicle parking solution that helps people find vacant parking spaces in the shortest amount of time.

    *

    Paka's Abilities

    It allows you to select a location of where you wish to go for the day. It allows you to book a parking space of the place you wish to go to. It gives you directions to the place. It allows you to pay for the parking on the app, so you do not wait in any queue while using Paka.

    The Value Added by our System

    We believe our Information system has added a value to the driving majority of our country. We believe it will reduce traffic congestions in parking areas as this system will reduce the time in finding vacant parking spaces. We believe it will eliminate the unknown factor of parking space availability. It adds an automated payment system based on time spent in the parking bay, so in conclusion it is a parking conflict resolver.

    These are the three main values we believe our system has impacted the most:

    • Parking Availibilty: Our users will have the power in their hands to find Parking easily.
    • Online Payment: Our users will have the ability to pay within the app without having to wait in any queue.
    • Time: Our users will not face any time consumptions while using this App, it will be a quick parking solution within their hands.
